Instant visualization for QGIS. Designed for students, beginners, and GIS professionals who need speed.

Why use this plugin?

Smart Detection

Automatically detects layers with numeric fields. No more scrolling through endless text fields.

Auto Colors

Pre-loaded with multiple professional color schemes (Reds, Blues, Spectral) for instant styling.

Robust Handling

NULL values are handled safely, ensuring your map never crashes due to missing data.

One Click

Select your field, pick a color, and click OK. The simplest workflow in QGIS.

How It Works

1

Load Layer

Open QGIS and load your vector/shapefile.

2

Open Plugin

Find the icon in the toolbar or Plugins menu.

3

Configure

Select the numeric field and your preferred color.

4

Done!

Click OK to generate the thematic map instantly.

Installation

01 Download the ZIP file from the button above.
02 Open QGIS → Plugins → Manage and Install.
03 Select "Install from ZIP" tab.
04 Browse file & Click "Install Plugin".
05 Enjoy One Click Thematic Mapping!
